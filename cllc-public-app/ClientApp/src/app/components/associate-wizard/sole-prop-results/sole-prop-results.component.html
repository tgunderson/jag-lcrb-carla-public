Instructions for Sole Proprietors

BC resident + is in B.C. + eligible for a BC Services Card

You will need
  • A Photo BC Services Card
  • Witness of Signature Attestation Form
  • Witness of Signature Attestation Form
  • Consent for Cannabis Security Screening Form
#1: Get a Photo BC Services Card
  • If you don’t have a card, see [URL] for instructions on getting one
#2: Prepare your forms:
  • Print the Witness of Signature Attestation Form and the Identity Verification Attestation Form. Do not complete either form. They must be completed by a Service BC representative.
  • Complete all fields on the Consent for Cannabis Security Screening Form except the signature and the date, then print the form. A Service BC representative must witness you completing the signature and date fields.
#3: Visit a Service BC location
  • Bring your forms and Photo BC Services Card
  • A Service BC representative will process your documents and send them to the Liquor and Cannabis Regulation Branch.

BC resident + is in B.C. + isn’t eligible for a BC Services Card

You will need
  • Witness of Signature Attestation Form
  • Identity Verification Attestation Form
  • Consent for Cannabis Security Screening
  • A piece of government-issued ID that contains your name, photo, date of birth and signature
  • A piece of secondary ID that contains your name and signature. The name must be imprinted and exactly match the name on your government-issued ID
#1: Prepare your forms:
  • Print the Witness of Signature Attestation Form and the Identity Verification Attestation Form. Do not complete either form. They must be completed by a Service BC representative.
  • Complete all fields on the Consent for Cannabis Security Screening Form except the signature and the date, then print the form. A Service BC representative must witness you completing the signature and date fields.
#2: Visit a Service BC location
  • Bring your forms and Photo BC Services Card
  • A Service BC representative will process your documents and send them to the Liquor and Cannabis Regulation Branch.

Canadian resident (non-B.C.) + is in B.C.

You will need

  • Identity Verification Attestation Form
  • Consent for Cannabis Security Screening
  • A piece of government-issued ID that contains your name, photo, date of birth and signature
  • A piece of secondary ID that contains your name and signature. The name must be imprinted and exactly match the name on your government-issued ID
#1: Prepare your forms:
  • Print the Witness of Signature Attestation Form and the Identity Verification Attestation Form. Do not complete either form. They must be completed by a Service BC representative.
  • Complete all fields on the Consent for Cannabis Security Screening Form except the signature and the date, then print the form. A Service BC representative must witness you completing the signature and date fields.

Canadian resident (non-BC) + isn’t in B.C.

You will need
  • Witness of Signature Attestation Form
  • Identity Verification Attestation Form
  • Consent for Cannabis Security Screening
  • A piece of government-issued ID that contains your name, photo, date of birth and signature
  • A piece of secondary ID that contains your name and signature. The name must be imprinted and exactly match the name on your government-issued ID
#1: Prepare your forms:
  • Print the Witness of Signature Attestation Form and the Identity Verification Attestation Form. Do not complete either form. They must be completed by a police station clerk.
  • Complete all fields on the Consent for Cannabis Security Screening Form except the signature and the date, then print the form. A police station clerk must witness you completing the signature and date fields.
  • Scan the front and back of your government-issued and secondary pieces of ID. Print the scans.
#2: Visit a local police station
  1. Bring your forms and your original two pieces of ID.
  2. Ask a police station clerk to:
    • Conduct a verification of both pieces of ID
    • Make sure your information on the Consent for Cannabis Security Screening Form matches your ID
    • Witness you sign and date the Consent for Cannabis Security Screening Form
    • Complete the Identity Verification Attestation Form
    • Complete the Witness of Signature Attestation Form
  3. Request a Police Information Check (PIC). Don’t open your PIC when you receive it.
#3: Submit your documents
  • Email your forms and scans of your ID to lcrbcannabis@gov.bc.ca. Include your name in the subject line.
  • Mail your unopened Police Information Check to the Liquor and Cannabis Regulation Branch
    • Mail your unopened Police Information Check to the Liquor and Cannabis Regulation Branch
    • Mailing address:
      Attn: Licensing Department
      Liquor and Cannabis Regulation Branch
      PO Box 9292 Stn Provincial Govt.
      Victoria, BC V8W 9J8

International Resident + is in B.C.

You will need
  • Witness of Signature Attestation Form
  • Identity Verification Attestation Form
  • Consent for Cannabis Security Screening form
  • Your passport
  • Another piece of government-issued ID that contains your name and signature. The name must be imprinted and exactly match the name on your passport.
  • The Reference Number of your cannabis retail store licence application (6-digit number found on your application payment receipt and in an email sent to you
#1: Prepare your forms
  • Print the Witness of Signature Attestation Form and the Identity Verification Attestation Form. Do not complete either form. They must be completed by a Service BC representative.
  • Complete all fields on the Consent for Cannabis Security Screening Form except the signature and the date, then print the form. A Service BC representative must witness you completing the signature and date fields.
#2: Visit a Service BC location
  • Bring your forms, passport and government-issued ID
  • A Service BC representative will process your documents and send them to the Liquor and Cannabis Regulation Branch
#3: Get a Criminal Record Check
  • Request a Criminal Record Check from your country of residency. If the Criminal Record Check isn’t going to be in English, request two copies.
  • When you receive your Criminal Record Check, don’t open it. If you’ve asked for two copies, leave one of them unopened. You’ll need to open the other one so it can be translated.
  • If your Criminal Record Check isn’t in English, have the opened copy translated into English. The translator must be admitted to a professional association. Do not open the translated copy when you receive it.
#4: Mail the original unopened copy of your Criminal Record Check and the unopened translated copy to the Liquor and Cannabis Regulation Branch
  • Include a note indicating the Reference Number of your cannabis retail store licence application
  • Mail your unopened Police Information Check to the Liquor and Cannabis Regulation Branch
    • Mail your unopened Police Information Check to the Liquor and Cannabis Regulation Branch
    • Mailing address:
      Attn: Licensing Department
      Liquor and Cannabis Regulation Branch
      PO Box 9292 Stn Provincial Govt.
      Victoria, BC V8W 9J8

International Resident + isn’t in B.C.

You will need
  • Witness of Signature Attestation Form
  • Identity Verification Attestation Form
  • Consent for Cannabis Security Screening Form
  • Your passport
  • Another piece of government-issued ID that contains your name and signature. The name must be imprinted and exactly match the name on your passport.
  • The Reference Number of your cannabis retail store licence application (6-digit number found on your application payment receipt and in an email sent to you)
#1: Prepare your forms and ID
  • Scan the front and back of the ID page in your passport. Print the scan.
  • Scan the front and back of your government-issued ID. Print the scan.
  • Print the Identity Verification Form. Do not complete the form. It must be completed by a police station clerk.
  • Print the Witness of Signature Attestation Form. Do not complete the form. It must be completed by a police station clerk, a lawyer or notary.
  • Complete all fields on the Consent for Cannabis Security Screening Form except the signature and the date, then print the form. A police station clerk, lawyer or notary must witness you completing the signature and date fields.
#2: Have your identity verified
  • Visit a local police station with the Identity Verification Attestation Form, your passport and your government-issued ID
  • Ask a police station clerk to:
    • Conduct an in-person verification of your passport and government-issued ID
    • Complete the Identity Verification Attestation Form
#3: Have your signature witnessed
  • Visit a local police station, lawyer or notary with:
    • Your passport
    • Your government-issued ID
    • Witness of Signature Attestation Form
    • Consent for Cannabis Security Screening Form
  • Ask the police station clerk, lawyer or notary to:
    • Verify that the information on your Cannabis Security Screening Form matches the information on your passport and government-issued ID
    • Witness you sign and date the Consent for Cannabis Security Screening Form
    • Complete the Witness of Signature Attestation Form
#4: Get a Criminal Record Check
  • Request a Criminal Record Check from your country of residency. If the Criminal Record Check isn’t going to be in English, request two copies.
  • When you receive your Criminal Record Check, don’t open it. If you’ve asked for two copies, leave one of them unopened. You’ll need to open the other one so it can be translated.
  • If your Criminal Record Check isn’t in English, have the opened copy translated into English. The translator must be admitted to a professional association. Do not open the translated copy when you receive it.
#3: Submit your documents
  • Email your forms and scans of your ID to lcrbcannabis@gov.bc.ca. Include your name in the subject line.
    • Identity Verification Attestation Form
    • Witness of Signature Attestation Form
    • Consent for Cannabis Security Screening Form
    • The scan of your passport’s ID page
    • The scan of the front and back of your government-issued ID
  • Mail your original, unopened Criminal Record Check and unopened translated copy to the Liquor and Cannabis Regulation Branch
    • Include a note indicating the Reference Number of your cannabis retail store licence application
    • Mailing address:
      Attn: Licensing Department
      Liquor and Cannabis Regulation Branch
      PO Box 9292 Stn Provincial Govt.
      Victoria, BC V8W 9J8
